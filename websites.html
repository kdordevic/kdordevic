Love pet - Veterinary clinic

This project was made for a veterinary practice Love pet in Belgrade. It was built with React, offering a smooth and user-friendly experience. Through the website, you can easily find detailed information about the practice and their services, make contact through email and social media and locate it on integrated map. The responsive design ensures accessibility on all devices, from smartphones to desktops. Visit the site

Petar Djordjevic - Artist's Portfolio

This site was created as a final project in the front-end development training at Interface 3. The front-end part was made in Angular, and the back-end in Node.js and MySql. The part accessible to all visitors includes the home page, the gallery, the about page and the contact page. Via the part of the site accessible only to the administrator, it is possible to modify the content of the gallery. Visit the site
